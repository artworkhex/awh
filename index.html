Hobby to Innovation



February 15, 2019, a talented artist named Alwan embarked on a creative journey by founding the startup, ArtworkHex. Initially a personal hobby, ArtworkHex now encompasses the fields of art, image manipulation, photography, videography, and coding.



ArtworkHex is a unique fusion of art and technology, combining aesthetic image manipulation, photography and videography skills, and innovation through coding. Built from dedication on February 15, 2019, ArtworkHex is not just a brand but a spirit of creativity that inspires.
